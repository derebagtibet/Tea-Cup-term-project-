# Tea Cup Term Project

## Overview
The Tea Cup Term Project aims to provide a comprehensive platform for tea enthusiasts to explore various types of tea, learn about their benefits, and find the best ways to brew them.

## Features
- **Tea Database**: A searchable database of various tea types, including green tea, black tea, herbal tea, and more.
- **Brewing Guide**: Step-by-step instructions on how to brew different types of tea for optimal flavor and health benefits.
- **User Reviews**: A section for users to leave reviews and ratings on different tea products and brewing methods.
- **Community Forum**: A platform for users to share their tea experiences, tips, and recommendations.
